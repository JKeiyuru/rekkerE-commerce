About Rekker Toys

Since 2010, Rekker Toys has been bringing joy and creativity to children and families across Kenya. What started as a passion for unique, high-quality toys has grown into a trusted brand, known for delivering a wide variety of products that inspire learning and play. Over the years, we've expanded beyond toys to offer kitchen utensils, school stationery, and specially curated gift packages, ensuring there's something for everyone, no matter the season.

At Rekker Toys, we pride ourselves on sourcing the best toys from around the world, with a focus on fun, education, and safety. Our toys are carefully selected to spark imagination and development, catering to children of all ages.

Why Shop with Us?

  • Quality You Can Trust: We've spent over a decade building strong relationships with reputable manufacturers, ensuring that every product meets the highest standards.
  • Variety: From classic toys to modern trends, kitchen essentials, and back-to-school must-haves, we're your one-stop shop.
  • Customer-Centric: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable experience.

As we continue to grow, our mission remains the same: to make life a little more fun, convenient, and memorable for our customers. Thank you for making Rekker Toys part of your family's journey, and we look forward to serving you for many years to come.
